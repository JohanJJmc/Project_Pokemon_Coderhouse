2nd Task

Arepas venezolanas

Ingredientes

  • 40 gr Salt
  • 200 gr flour P.A.N
  • 30 gr sugar
  • 200 ml water
  • bowl
  • 60 gr of butter
  • Stuffing of your choice: cheese, ham, pork, beans, shredded meat, etc.

Preparation

  1. Add in the wol the 200 grams of flour, you can sift it if you prefer
  2. Add water homogeneously
  3. Mix the flour with the water until a solid and smooth dough is formed, avoiding a powdery consistency.
  4. Make balls with the dough of the hand size and then flatten leaving a maximum thickness of 1 cm.
  5. Put the frying pan over medium heat.
  6. Place the flattened and circular dough on the frying pan previously buttered.
  7. Remove your arepa in 7 min and place your Stuffing.
